Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Information We Collect

When you use CallRabbit, we collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, phone number, and any messages you send through our contact form or voice interactions.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our AI voice and text agent services
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • To send you service-related communications
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our platform

Voice and Call Data

CallRabbit processes voice interactions to deliver our AI agent services. Call recordings and transcripts are stored securely, encrypted at rest and in transit, and retained only as long as necessary to provide the service. You may request deletion of your call data at any time.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers who help us operate our platform (hosting, analytics, voice synthesis), and only under strict data processing agreements.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, access controls, and regular security audits. See our Security page for details.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at info@callrabbit.ai.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to operate the platform. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or trackers.
